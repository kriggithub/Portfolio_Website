* **Vibe/Style:** Minimalist, modern, and sleek. Inspired by the **Apple website** (clean, impactful, with a focus on high-quality presentation).

## 5. Technology Stack Recommendations
* **Current Preference:** Open to suggestions.

This stack is perfect for building premium, modern web experiences.
